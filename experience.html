Steps to take before writing a computer program

  • Identify the problem:What problem does your program solve? If you can’t clearly state what your program does, you won’t know how to design it.
  • Determine the target computer:Which computer do people need to run your program? Is it a Windows computer, a Macintosh, a mainframe, a computer running Linux, a handheld Palm or Pocket PC, or a supercomputer?
  • Identify the user:Who’s going to use your program?
  • Determine your programming skill: Are you going to write the entire thing yourself or get help from others? If you’re going to get others to help you, which parts of the program are they going to write?
